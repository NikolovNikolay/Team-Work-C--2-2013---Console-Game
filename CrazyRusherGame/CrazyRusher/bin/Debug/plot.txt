"Crazy Rusher Game" consists of a matrix, dungeon maze, known as
"The maze", which is being unhabited by swarm of evil creatures,
known as "The evil creatures". It is generally accepted, that 
peace and happiness always reign over the forces of evil, so our
main character "Smiley" is being teleported to the dungeon, to
destroy the swarm, before they turn the maze into an infectious 
hive, and populate the world with bloodthirsty dummies. 

                                     - Good luck, Smiley!
